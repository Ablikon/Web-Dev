Travel to Paris

Basic Info

Paris is one of the world's most iconic cities and the capital of France. Known as the "City of Light," it's renowned for its art, culture, fashion, and gastronomy.
The city attracts millions of visitors each year and is home to some of the world's most famous landmarks, including the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum.

More Info

Paris has an excellent public transportation system that includes the Metro, buses, and RER trains. The Paris Metro is one of the oldest and largest metro systems in the world, serving millions of passengers daily.
You can easily pay for transport using Navigo cards, contactless bank cards, or mobile payments throughout the city.
